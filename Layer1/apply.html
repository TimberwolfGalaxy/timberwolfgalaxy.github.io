A Clean Record

In order to protect the community, we require that any applicant has a clean record, which is defined as a record with no punishments excluding warnings in the last twelve months (or less depending on the age of the record). This also helps us ensure that the applicant has a clear and good unserstanding of server rules.

Experience as a Player

So that all our staff has experience being a player and understanding more about what the player wants, we require that all our staff have graduated in game.

[MOD AND ADMIN APP ONLY] Previous Staff Experience

So that we already know you, we require that people applying to be moderators have at least three months of experience as a staff member of a lower level (professor, trainer, developer, or builder). Applicants for the administrator position MUST have had experience as a moderator for three months prior to applying.



We believe that age of our staff does not matter, however, staff are only allowed to request salary if their legal age is above fourteen to comply with the minimum age requirement for employment in the US.

PROCESS

STEP 1: APPLICATION

Click the 'Apply' button at the top of the page to be directed to our staff application, and fill it out as accurately as possible.

STEP 2: WAIT FOR A RESPONSE

Applications should be reviewed and responded to within two weeks of submission. If you don't get a response, feel free to shoot us a Discord message HERE. It's okay, we don't bite. If accepted, you can go on to become staff. If not, apply again, but wait a month before you apply so we have time to go through all the other applications that come in!

STEP 3: DO YOUR DUTIES

We want our server's staff to be active. Get online at least once a week for an hour so we know you're still there and that you're not dead.

STEP 4 [OPTIONAL]: REQUEST PAYMENT

We want to compensate you the best we can for the work you do, but it's really hard for us to pay our staff because the animals really need the money. However, if you need the money too, we could occasionally try to accomodate a small cut for you, however we make no promises.



YOU MUST BE AT LEAST 14 YEARS OF AGE TO REQUEST PAYMENT
